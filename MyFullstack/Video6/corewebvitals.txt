CLS:- Cumulative Layout Shift
-----------------------------
means page load hote hote kitneshift ho raha hai

LCP:- Largest Contentful Paint
_______________________________
means website mein sabse bada dikhne wala content kitne der main load ho raha hai.

According to the google- good lcp value is 2.5 for a website

FID:-First Input Delay
_______________________
mean agar kisibhi user ne agar kuchh input diya or button click kiya to kitna der leta hai process hone main. 

ideal=100ms
